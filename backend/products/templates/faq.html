Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about orders, shipping, payment, and your account.

Orders & Shipping

You can track your order anytime from the "My Orders" page in your account. It shows the current status of each order, from pending to delivered.

You can cancel an order from "My Orders" as long as it is still in the pending state. Once it has been shipped, cancellation is no longer possible.

If you receive a damaged or incorrect product, please contact us within 3 days of delivery through our Contact Us page, and we'll help resolve it.
